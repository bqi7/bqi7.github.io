ABOUT ME
HI THERE!

I’m Brian, a junior at the University of California, Berkeley majoring in Computer Science and Data Science. I am a US born Kiwi who loves to travel - having lived in various countries such as China, UK and New Zealand over the past two decades.

At Berkeley, I am a software developer for Pioneers in Engineering. I am also very passionate about STEM education and currently work as a Computer Science Mentor to help students succeed in lower division courses.

I like to spend my free time playing squash, eating hotpot and trying different milkshake flavors. Feel free to reach out to me - I’d love to chat!
